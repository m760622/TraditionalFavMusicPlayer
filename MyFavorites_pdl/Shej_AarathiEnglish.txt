2

Arati Gyanaraja mahakaivalya teja

Sewiti sadhu sant, manu vedhala majha

Lopale gyan jagi, hit nenti koni

Avatar panduranga, nam thevile gyani

Narad tumbaraho, saam gaayan kari

Pragat guhya bole, vishwa brahmachi kele

3

Satchidanand murti, Pay dakhavi amha

Raghave sagarant, jaise pashan tareele

Taise he tukobache abhang (udaki) raksheele

Tukita tulnesi, brahma tukasi alle

Mhanoni rameshware, Charani mastak thevile.

4

Jai jai sainath ata pahudawe mandiri ho

Alvito sapreme tujala arati ghevooni kariho jai…..

Ranjavisi tu madhur boluni maya jaishinija mulaho

Bhogisi vyadhi tucha haruniya nija sevak dukhala ho

Dhavuni bhakta vyasan harisi darshan desi tyala ho

Jahle astil kasht ateeshaya tumche ya dehala ho-jai….

Shama shayan sundar hee shobha sumanshej tyavari ho

Ghyavi thodi bhakta jananchi poojanadi chakari ho

Owalito panch pran jyoti sumati kari ho

Seva kinkar bhaktapriti attar parimal vari ho-jai….

Soduni jaaya dukh watate sai tvacharanasi ho

Agnestav tav ashiprasad ghevooni nij sadanasi ho

Jato ata yevoo punarapi twa charanache pashi ho

Uthavoo tujala sai mavoole nijahit sadhayasi ho-jai….
